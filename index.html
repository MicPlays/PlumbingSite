About

Mechanical Wizards was established in 1994. We provide full plumbing and heating services for multi-family, commercial, and residential buildings, as well as remodels. We have many years of experience building dental offices, hotels, townhomes, apartments, condos, and commercial office buildings. We specialize in sawcutting, pipe insulation, installation of cast iron, copper, and PVC pipe, fire caulking, P2904 fire suppresion installation, air, vent, and vacuum systems, storm water management, and water and sewer utilities. We are licensed and insured, and will provide free quotes upon request.
